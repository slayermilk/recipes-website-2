Main Course

3 Sauce Chicken

Full Chicken Marinate with adrak lehsen paste, lemon juice, green chili paste, salt and keep it overnight for good results. Put it in a saucepan n let it cook on slow flame, turn it once. Once colour changes, remove from flame. Keep the sage aside. Then fry it in oil and keep aside. Boil macaroni and keep aside. For Red Sauce: take tomato sauce, dahi, lehsen, oil, salt and add Chilli Garlic too, if you want and blend it. White Sauce: maida, milk, salt, oregano, lehsen, barik pyaz and make it as you make for Lasagne. For Vegetables: Saute capsicum, pyaz cut in 4s, carrots and cauliflower to be steamed, with soya sauce, vinegar, salt, sugar and Worcester if you want to... Add the boiled macaroni to the veggies. Assemble the veggies n chicken in a dish and grill it in the oven. For Green Sauce: Blend green chili with sarka n lehsen n zeera.

Chicken Chilli

Wash murghi....n add adrak lehsen n haldi to marinate..do not add salt. Cut onions, capsicum in square pieces n keep aside. Make a paste of kashmiri mirch, lehsen, 1 green chili .....keep aside. In chicken stock add...soya sauce, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, salt, sugar, corn flour. Now in hot oil add murghi...fry it n remove...then add onions...fry awhile n add capsicum. Fry n add murghi along with the kashmiri paste. Mix well, cover n let it cook. When tender, add the soya sauce mixture n mix well....garnish with spring onion n serve hot with chapati or rice.

Peene Ki Haleem

Peene ki haleem Boil gehunn bamix it Prepare hara masala ...hara dhania, lehsen n hari mirch Boil gosht adding namak n adrak lehsen... keep extra water Baghar with Kari patta, pyaz, hara masala n add boiled gehun n gosht...add water as required

Khawsa

Noodles 1 packet Chicken...small pieces 1kg 3/4 onions paste Lehsen....6/7 cloves barik Coconut milk...as required Besan as required Chilli powder as required Salt to taste Oil For garnish: Hari pyaz Kothmir Lehsen Tala hua Roti Tali hui Barista Method...take atleast 2 tablespoon oil, add lehsen once heated, then add onion paste, add a little salt n chilli powder too...saute till pyaz becomes pinkish, add chicken with adrak lehsen, chilli powder and salt.... Let it cook for awhile then add coconut milk n bring it to boil..then add water according to the gravy you want...let it boil n add besan mixed with water...lower the flame and let it cook...do not cover fully..taste salt n mirch...you can add more if needed. Check the gravy... add more besan if not thick enough. Prepare the garnish. I use the oil of the Tala hua lehsen by adding chilli powder for the chilli sauce.

Singaporean Rice

Chicken boneless Qeema for kabab Capsicum Hari pyaz Normal pyaz..cut in 4 pieces Gajar Corn Band gobhi Boil rice like this for biryani n add star anise... Marinate chicken with soya sauce, worcestershire sauce, chilly flakes, salt, sugar, tomato sauce, chili garlic accordingly. Optional make kache qeeme ke kabab Cut all veges n add oil in a pan....saute garlic add chicken, cook for awhile, add normal pyaz saute n then add capsicum, corn n gajar...let it cook. Once done add band gobhi Fry garlic n in the leftover oil add chilli powder. For sauce: Mayonnaise Dahi Chilli flakes Tomato without skin Salt sugar Vinegar Tomato n chilli garlic sauce Blend altogether. Set the dish Rice with vegetables on top Put sage as well Kababs surrounding it Garnish with Hari pyaz n dhania
